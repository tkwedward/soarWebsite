Through curriculum aligned with state standards, the Academy strives to prepare students to become responsible citizens in the 21st century, a world of cultural diversity and rapid technological change. Students are offered a challenging curriculum featuring Treasures Language Arts, enVision Mathematics and Science and McGraw Hill Social Studies.
